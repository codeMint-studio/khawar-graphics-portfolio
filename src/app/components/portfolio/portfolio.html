About Me

Hi, I'm Khawar Aziz

A passionate and dedicated Graphic Designer with over 4 years of professional experience crafting impactful visual designs that tell compelling stories. My journey has been shaped by collaborations with diverse national and international organizations, as well as individual clients across multiple industries.

These partnerships have refined my creative process, broadened my design perspective, and empowered me to deliver work that truly resonates. Starting with local teams and community initiatives, I've evolved my understanding of brand identity, digital presence, and contemporary design standards.

Today, I deliver clean, meaningful, and result-oriented visuals that represent brands in their best light—creating designs that don't just look good, but drive real impact.

Work with E-Kashmir

I am also the founder of E-Kashmir, a platform created with the vision of empowering and supporting the people of Kashmir. Through E-Kashmir, I actively work—completely free of cost—to spread awareness, promote local talent, and create opportunities for youth across the region.

This initiative allows me to use my skills for a greater purpose and contribute to the betterment of our community.
