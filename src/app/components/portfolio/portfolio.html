About Me

I'm Khawar Aziz, a passionate and dedicated Graphic Designer with 4+ years of professional experience in creating impactful visual designs. Over the years, I've worked with various national and international organizations, as well as individual clients from different industries.

These collaborations have strengthened my creative approach, expanded my design vision, and helped me deliver work that truly stands out. I began my journey by working with local teams and community-driven initiatives, and as my experience grew, so did my ability to understand brand identity, digital presence, and modern design standards.

Today, I am confident in delivering clean, meaningful, and result-oriented visuals that represent brands in the best way possible.

Work with E-Kashmir

I am also the founder of E-Kashmir, a platform created with the vision of empowering and supporting the people of Kashmir. Through E-Kashmir, I actively work—completely free of cost—to spread awareness, promote local talent, and create opportunities for youth across the region.

This initiative allows me to use my skills for a greater purpose and contribute to the betterment of our community.
